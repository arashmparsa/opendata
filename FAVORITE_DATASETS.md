# Favorite Datasets of Interest

These are my preferred open data sources for analysis and visualization projects.

---

## Demographics & Census
1. **American Community Survey (ACS) 5-Year Estimates** - Detailed demographic, social, economic, and housing data
2. **Decennial Census Summary Files** - Complete population counts every 10 years
3. **Current Population Survey (CPS) Microdata** - Labor force and employment statistics
4. **County Business Patterns** - Business establishment data by geography and industry
5. **Longitudinal Employer-Household Dynamics (LEHD)** - Employment and earnings linked data

---

## Health
6. **Medicare Provider Utilization and Payment Data** - Healthcare provider billing and payment info
7. **Hospital Compare Quality Measures** - Hospital quality ratings and metrics
8. **CDC WONDER Mortality Data** - Death statistics and causes
9. **FDA Adverse Event Reporting System (FAERS)** - Drug and medical device adverse events
10. **NHANES Survey Data** - National health and nutrition examination surveys
11. **COVID-19 Case Surveillance** - Pandemic tracking and case data
12. **Medicaid Drug Utilization** - Prescription drug usage in Medicaid

---

## Financial
13. **HMDA Mortgage Data** - Home mortgage disclosure data (billions of records)
14. **SBA Loan Data** - Small Business Administration 7(a) and 504 loan programs
15. **CFPB Consumer Complaint Database** - Consumer financial complaints
16. **SEC EDGAR Filings** - Securities and Exchange Commission structured filings
17. **FDIC Failed Banks List** - Historical bank failures
18. **USASpending Federal Contracts** - Federal government spending and contracts

---

## Economic
29. **Bureau of Labor Statistics (BLS)** - Employment, wages, inflation, productivity
30. **Bureau of Economic Analysis (BEA)** - GDP, trade, regional economic accounts
31. **Federal Reserve Economic Data (FRED)** - Comprehensive economic time series
32. **Import/Export Trade Data** - International trade statistics
33. **Producer/Consumer Price Indices** - Inflation and price change tracking

---

## Scientific
46. **PubChem Chemical Data** - Chemical compound information
47. **GenBank Sequences** - Genetic sequence database
48. **Landsat Satellite Imagery** - Earth observation satellite images
49. **USGS Earthquake Catalog** - Seismic event records
50. **NASA Earth Observation Data** - Climate and environmental satellite data
